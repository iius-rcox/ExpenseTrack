# Signing In

Sign in to ExpenseFlow using your company Microsoft credentials.

## Overview

ExpenseFlow uses Microsoft Single Sign-On (SSO), which means you use the same username and password as your company email and other Microsoft 365 applications. No separate account creation is needed.

## How to Sign In

1. Navigate to your company's ExpenseFlow URL in your web browser
2. Click the **Sign in with Microsoft** button

   ![Sign in button](../images/dashboard/sign-in-screen.png)
   *Caption: The sign-in page with the Microsoft authentication button*

3. Enter your company email address (e.g., yourname@company.com)
4. Enter your password
5. Complete any additional authentication (if your company uses multi-factor authentication)
6. You'll be redirected to your ExpenseFlow dashboard

> **Tip**: Bookmark the ExpenseFlow URL for quick access.

## First-Time Sign In

If this is your first time accessing ExpenseFlow:

1. Complete the sign-in steps above
2. You'll see a welcome message on the dashboard
3. Your account is automatically configured with your:
   - Name (from your Microsoft account)
   - Email address
   - Default department (if pre-assigned by your administrator)

No additional setup is required to start using ExpenseFlow.

## Signing Out

To sign out of ExpenseFlow:

1. Click your profile icon in the top-right corner
2. Select **Sign Out** from the dropdown menu
3. You'll be redirected to the sign-in page

> **Note**: Signing out of ExpenseFlow does not sign you out of other Microsoft applications.

## Session Duration

Your sign-in session remains active while you use ExpenseFlow. The session may expire after:

- Extended periods of inactivity (typically 30-60 minutes)
- Your browser is closed
- You manually sign out

If your session expires, simply sign in again. Any unsaved work may need to be re-entered.

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Sign In

If you're unable to sign in:

1. Verify your email address is correct
2. Check that your password is correct (try signing into another Microsoft app)
3. If your password was recently changed, use the new password
4. Contact your IT administrator if problems persist

### "Access Denied" Message

If you see an access denied error:

1. Your account may not be authorized for ExpenseFlow
2. Contact your ExpenseFlow administrator to request access
3. They'll need to provision your account in the system

### Browser Compatibility

ExpenseFlow works best with modern browsers:

- Google Chrome (recommended)
- Mozilla Firefox
- Apple Safari
- Microsoft Edge

If you experience issues, try updating your browser or switching to Chrome.
