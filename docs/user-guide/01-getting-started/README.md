# Getting Started

Welcome to ExpenseFlow! This section guides you through your first steps with the application, from signing in to uploading your first receipt.

## In This Section

| Guide | Description | Time |
|-------|-------------|------|
| [Signing In](./signing-in.md) | Authenticate with your company credentials | 2 min |
| [Dashboard Overview](./dashboard-overview.md) | Understand your workspace and pending tasks | 5 min |
| [Quick Start](./quick-start.md) | Upload your first receipt and see AI extraction | 5 min |

## Before You Begin

Make sure you have:
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- Your company Microsoft account credentials
- At least one receipt (photo or PDF) ready to upload

## Recommended Reading Order

1. **[Signing In](./signing-in.md)** - Start here to access ExpenseFlow
2. **[Dashboard Overview](./dashboard-overview.md)** - Learn where everything is
3. **[Quick Start](./quick-start.md)** - Upload your first receipt

## What You'll Learn

After completing this section, you'll be able to:
- Sign in using Microsoft Single Sign-On (SSO)
- Navigate the dashboard and understand pending actions
- Upload a receipt and review AI-extracted data
- Understand confidence scores (green/amber/red indicators)

## Estimated Time

**10-15 minutes** to complete all guides in this section.
